About Me

My Name is Benjamin Nodland. I was born in Seoul South Korea and was adopted to a family in Minneapolis Minnesota. After an uneventful childhood I signed up to join the military my senior year of High School. This was shortly after Sept 11th, 2001. I have been stationed several places around the globe over my 20 years of service.
Portland Oregon is my current home and will probably be my last duty station before retirement. I have a great wife and dog that keep me company.
